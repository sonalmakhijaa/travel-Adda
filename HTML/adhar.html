"Adharshila" Stone Art Festival

This November, celebrate the age-old art of stone sculpting at "Adharshila", a unique and Madhya Pradesh's first stone art festival, being organised under One District One Product (ODOP) programme. The event will invite sculptors from across India to work and present their creations on sandstone and marble. Sculptures created during the festival will be displayed during an exhibition-cum-auction on the closing day. The event will be held from 9th to 28th November, 2021, at Jagriti Park, Katni .
