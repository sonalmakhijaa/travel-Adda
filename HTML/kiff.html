Khajuraho International Film Festival (KIFF)

Organized by the Government of Madhya Pradesh in collaboration with Prayaas Production Pvt Ltd, KIFF is the first film festival at a World Heritage site in India. Khajuraho International Film Festival (KIFF) has been organized for the past six years at Khajuraho, the biggest tourist center of Madhya Pradesh. The KIFF's founding principles center on the discovery, promotion, and support of film-making in all genres, thus bringing together the diversity of forms, aesthetics, and content.
